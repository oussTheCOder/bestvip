## Before You Start

Before activating IPTV on your Smart TV, your provider will usually send you one of these login formats:

- **Xtream Codes API:** username, password, and server URL.
- **M3U URL:** one playlist link that contains your IPTV channels.
- **Device ID, Device Key, or MAC Address:** used by apps like IBO Player and Ibo Player Pro.

## Common IPTV Activation Problems

### The App Is Not Available on My Smart TV

Some apps are not available in every country or on every TV model. If you cannot find the app on your LG TV, Samsung TV, or other Smart TV, you can use an external device such as:

- Android TV box
- Fire Stick
- Google TV
- Android smartphone or tablet

### My Channels Are Not Loading

If your channels are not loading, check the following:

- Your internet connection is stable.
- Your username and password are correct.
- Your server URL is copied correctly.
- Your M3U URL is complete.
- Your subscription or trial test is still active.

If you are using Streambe, contact support here: [Streambe IPTV support](https://wa.me/19295664890?text=Hello%20Streambe%2C%20my%20IPTV%20channels%20are%20not%20loading).

### The Playlist Is Saved but Nothing Appears

If the playlist is saved but channels do not appear, restart the app completely.

You can also restart your TV and router, then open the app again.

For IBO Player and Ibo Player Pro, make sure you used the correct Device ID, Device Key, or MAC Address.

### The App Asks for Activation

IBO Player and Ibo Player Pro may require app activation after the trial period. This activation is separate from your IPTV subscription.

Your IPTV provider gives you the playlist or subscription details. The app activation is managed by the app provider.
